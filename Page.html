About Gen-Z IT

GenZ IT is an educational institution dedicated to providing training in essential modern skills such as Content Writing, Graphic Design, Web Design, and Digital Marketing. With the motto "Learn Today, Lead Tomorrow," they aim to equip learners with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in their careers. GenZ IT boasts a team of experienced trainers and offers flexible learning options to suit various schedules. They emphasize job assurance through job placement assistance and provide comprehensive career guidance to help students achieve their professional goals. Located at Road 4, Dhanmondi, Dhaka, with an additional branch in Kushtia, GenZ IT ensures accessibility for students in different regions. Additionally, they offer 100% paid internship opportunities upon course completion, enabling students to gain valuable practical experience.
